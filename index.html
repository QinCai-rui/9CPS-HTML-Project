Welcome to Kupe House

Kindness • Understanding • Perseverance • Enthusiasm

Named after the great Polynesian navigator who discovered Aotearoa, Kupe House has the spirit of exploration and (arguably more important for school life) achievement.

About Kupe House

Kupe House is one of the whanau houses at Macleans College. Our house provides a supportive environment where students develop academically, socially, and personally throughout their time at Macleans.

Our house color is GOLD, and our mascot is the kiwi, representing our connection to Aotearoa, just like Kupe himself.
