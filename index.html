Welcome to Kupe House

Kindness • Understanding • Perseverance • Enthusiasm

Named after the great Polynesian navigator who discovered Aotearoa, Kupe House has the spirit of exploration and (arguably more important for school life) achievement.

About Kupe House

Kupe House is one of the eight whanau houses at Macleans College.

"This is a house full of explorers and adventurers, a house full of promise and highly motivated young people."

"There is a real sense of whānau (family) in this house, fostered by hard working staff and students, all of whom enjoy being part of a warm, caring atmosphere where they are supported and encouraged to strive for excellence in everything they do."

Our house color is GOLD. Our mascot is the kiwi, representing our connection to Aotearoa New Zealand, just like Kupe himself.
